## ğŸ—£ï¸ **Presentation Tips**

### **Before You Start:**
1. **Test your demo**: Make sure `localhost:8001/visual-demo.html` works
2. **Practice transitions**: Smooth switch between slides and live demo
3. **Prepare for questions**: Review the technical details
4. **Time yourself**: Aim for 5-7 minutes total

### **During Presentation:**
1. **Start confident**: You built something amazing!
2. **Make eye contact**: Connect with your audience
3. **Speak clearly**: Don't rush through technical parts
4. **Show enthusiasm**: Your passion for pets and tech should shine
5. **Handle questions calmly**: It's okay to say "Great question, let me show you..."

### **Demo Best Practices:**
1. **Have backup**: Screenshots in case demo fails
2. **Zoom browser**: Make sure everyone can see
3. **Narrate actions**: "Now I'm clicking add to cart..."
4. **Show mobile**: Resize browser to demonstrate responsiveness
5. **Keep moving**: Don't get stuck on one feature
